About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Chemistry at the University of Pennsylvania, working with Prof. Abraham Nitzan. My research focuses on quantum dynamics and cavity quantum electrodynamics, with an emphasis on how confined light-matter interactions reshape chemical reactivity, photophysics, and transport.

I received my M.S. and Ph.D. in Chemistry from the University of Rochester (advisor: Prof. Pengfei Huo) in 2025. Before that, I obtained my B.S. in Chemical Physics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).
